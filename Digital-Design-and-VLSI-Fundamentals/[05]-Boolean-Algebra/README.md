# 05. Boolean Algebra

[![Stage](https://img.shields.io/badge/Digital--Design--and--VLSI--Fundamentals-blue.svg)](#)
[![Focus](https://img.shields.io/badge/Focus-Boolean%20Algebra-green.svg)](#)

This module introduces the fundamental principles of Boolean Algebra, the mathematical foundation of digital electronics and logic circuit design. It covers Boolean operations, laws, theorems, and expression simplification techniques that are essential for designing, analyzing, and optimizing digital logic circuits used in Verilog HDL, RTL Design, FPGA Development, and VLSI Design.

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

By working through this module, you will be able to:

- Understand the fundamentals of Boolean Algebra and logical operations.
- Apply Boolean laws and identities to simplify logic expressions.
- Use De Morgan's Theorems to transform Boolean expressions.
- Analyze and simplify Boolean expressions for efficient digital circuit design.
- Build a strong foundation for combinational logic design and logic optimization.

## 🛠️ Core Concepts Covered

### 1. Boolean Basics

Learn the fundamental concepts of Boolean Algebra, including binary variables, logical operators, truth tables, and their role in digital systems.

### 2. Boolean Laws

Study the standard Boolean laws used to simplify logical expressions and reduce hardware complexity in digital circuit design.

### 3. De Morgan's Theorems

Understand De Morgan's First and Second Theorems and learn how they are used to transform and simplify Boolean expressions during logic implementation.

### 4. Boolean Expressions

Learn how to represent, evaluate, and simplify Boolean expressions to develop efficient combinational logic circuits.
